NP Membership

  • Free access to networking events and conferences.
  • Participation in training programs and specialized workshops.
  • Legal and tax advice for non-profit organizations.
  • Collaboration and networking opportunities with other similar organizations.
  • Exclusive newsletter with news and resources relevant to the nonprofit sector.

Bronze Membership

  • Access to exclusive events of the Lima Chamber of Commerce.
  • Basic advertising on the Chamber's website.
  • Participation in working groups and specialized committees.
  • Discounts on rates for consulting and training services.
  • Access to economic reports and statistics relevant to the sector.

Silver Membership

  • Priority access to training events and seminars.
  • Featured advertising in the Chamber's electronic newsletter.
  • Participation in trade missions and international fairs.
  • Technical assistance on export and internationalization issues.
  • Access to mediation and commercial conflict resolution services.

Gold Membership

  • Exclusive access to VIP events and meetings with business leaders.
  • Featured advertising on the home page of the Chamber website.
  • Personalized advice on strategic and management issues.
  • Special discounts on Chamber services and affiliated partners.
  • Access to personalized research reports and market analysis.
